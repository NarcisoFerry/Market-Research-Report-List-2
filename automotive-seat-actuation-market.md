ESAS utilizes electric motors for adjustments, providing convenience and precise positioning with features like memory settings and integrated controls. Both systems cater to varying consumer preferences, with ESAS gaining popularity due to its comfort and advanced functionality, while MSAS retains appeal for budget-conscious buyers.</p></p>
