<p><p>The Abrasive Waterjet Cutting Machine market features two primary types: Cantilever and Gantry. Cantilever machines are characterized by a single arm that supports the cutting head, allowing for flexibility and compact design, making them ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, Gantry machines utilize a bridge structure for stability, providing larger cutting areas and the ability to handle heavier materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, offering precision and versatility in cutting processes.</p></p>
