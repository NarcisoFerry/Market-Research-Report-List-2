<p><p>The radiation curable coatings market encompasses two primary types: UV-curable and electron beam (EB) curable coatings. UV-curable coatings utilize ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, resulting in rapid curing and high durability, making them ideal for various applications, including printing and coatings. Electron beam curable coatings, on the other hand, employ electron beams to induce curing, offering superior penetration and efficiency for thicker coatings. Both types provide environmentally friendly options, reducing solvent emissions and enhancing production speed in various industries.</p></p>
