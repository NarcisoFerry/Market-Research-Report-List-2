<p><p>The laser amplifiers market is divided into two primary types: pulsed laser amplifiers and continuous-wave (CW) laser amplifiers. Pulsed laser amplifiers are designed to deliver energy in short bursts, making them ideal for applications like material processing and medical procedures. In contrast, continuous-wave laser amplifiers provide a steady output of light, suitable for telecommunications, industrial applications, and research. Each type has distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of various industries and technological applications.</p></p>
