In contrast, synthetic rubber, made from petroleum-based materials, provides enhanced flexibility, resistance to moisture, and lower cost options, appealing to budget-conscious consumers. Each type serves varying needs in gyms, studios, and home fitness spaces, balancing performance, environmental impact, and economic factors.</p></p>
