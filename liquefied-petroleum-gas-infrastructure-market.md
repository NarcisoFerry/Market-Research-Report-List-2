<p><p>The Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) infrastructure market comprises key segments like liquefaction terminals and regasification terminals. Liquefaction terminals are facilities where LPG is converted from gaseous to liquid form, enabling efficient transportation and storage. Conversely, regasification terminals handle the process of converting LPG back into a gaseous state before distribution. Both types of terminals are vital for ensuring a reliable supply chain, facilitating trade, and supporting energy markets by providing flexible options for handling LPG across various regions.</p></p>
