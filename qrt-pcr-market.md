<p><strong>The qRT-PCR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>qPCR</li><li>dPCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The qRT-PCR market can be segmented into two primary types: quantitative PCR (qPCR) and digital PCR (dPCR). qPCR is widely used for quantifying gene expression and detecting specific DNA sequences, providing real-time data during the amplification process. In contrast, dPCR offers greater sensitivity and precision by partitioning samples into separate reactions, allowing for absolute quantification without the need for standard curves. Both methods serve essential roles in research, diagnostics, and various applications in molecular biology.</p></p>
