<p><p>The nuclear power reactors market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR)**: Uses pressurized water as both coolant and moderator.</p><p>2. **Boiling Water Reactor (BWR)**: Boils water to generate steam directly within the reactor vessel.</p><p>3. **Pressurized Heavy Water Reactor (PHWR)**: Employs heavy water as a moderator and coolant, allowing the use of natural uranium fuel.</p><p>4. **Fast Breeder Reactor (FBR)**: Breeds fuel while generating energy using fast neutrons.</p><p>5. **Light Water Graphite Reactor (LWR)**: Combines light water for cooling with graphite for moderation.</p><p>6. **Other market**: Encompasses emerging technologies and alternative reactor designs.</p></p>
