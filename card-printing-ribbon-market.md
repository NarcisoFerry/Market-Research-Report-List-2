<p><p>The card printing ribbon market primarily consists of two types: full color printing ribbons and monochrome printing ribbons. Full color printing ribbons utilize a combination of dye films to produce vibrant, high-resolution images and graphics, making them ideal for ID cards, badges, and promotional materials. In contrast, monochrome printing ribbons typically print in a single color, such as black or gold, focusing on text, barcodes, or simple images, which is cost-effective for straightforward designs and high-volume printing needs.</p></p>
