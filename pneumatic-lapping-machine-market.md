<p><p>The pneumatic lapping machine market is categorized into three main types: Semi-Automatic, Fully Automatic, and CNC types. Fully Automatic machines operate independently, handling all lapping tasks without human intervention, enhancing efficiency. CNC lapping machines utilize computer numerical control for precise, programmable operations, offering high accuracy and batch consistency. Together, these types cater to diverse manufacturing needs in various industries.</p></p>
