<p><p>The fundamental cylinder market includes various types designed for specific applications. Standard double-acting cylinders utilize pressure for movement in both directions. Single-acting cylinders operate using pressure in one direction and a spring return. Double-rod cylinders offer equal force in both directions with a rod extending from each end. Spring return cylinders use a spring mechanism for retraction. Ram type cylinders excel in high force applications, while telescoping cylinders feature nested tubes for extended reach. Tandem and duplex cylinders combine multiple cylinders for greater force and complex operations.</p></p>
