<p><strong>The Data Center Liquid Immersion Cooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Phase Cooling</li><li>Two Phase Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data center liquid immersion cooling market comprises two main types: single-phase cooling and two-phase cooling. Single-phase cooling involves submerging servers in a liquid that remains in a liquid state, efficiently transferring heat away from the components. In contrast, two-phase cooling utilizes a liquid that evaporates upon heat absorption, creating a cooling effect as it condenses back. Both methods improve energy efficiency and thermal management, catering to the increasing demands for high-performance computing in modern data centers.</p></p>
