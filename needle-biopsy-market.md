<p><strong>The Needle Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fine-Needle Aspiration Biopsy</li><li>Core Needle Biopsy</li><li>Vacuum-Assisted Biopsy</li><li>Image-Guided Biopsy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle biopsy market comprises various types, each serving distinct purposes in medical diagnostics. Fine-Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB) utilizes a thin needle for sampling cells from lumps, offering minimal invasiveness. Core Needle Biopsy (CNB) employs a larger needle to extract tissue cores, providing more comprehensive pathology. Vacuum-Assisted Biopsy (VAB) utilizes suction to collect larger tissue samples, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Image-Guided Biopsy leverages imaging techniques to precisely locate tissue abnormalities, ensuring targeted sampling and improving diagnostic outcomes.</p></p>
