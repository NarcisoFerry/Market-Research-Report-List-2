<p><p>The portable dishwasher market comprises primarily two types: spray-type dishwashers and ultrasonic-type dishwashers. Spray-type dishwashers utilize high-pressure jets to remove food residues and dirt from dishes, making them efficient for small spaces. Alternatively, ultrasonic-type dishwashers use high-frequency sound waves to generate microscopic bubbles that implode, creating a gentle yet thorough cleaning action. Both types cater to the needs of consumers seeking compact, effective cleaning solutions, particularly in urban settings where space is limited.</p></p>
