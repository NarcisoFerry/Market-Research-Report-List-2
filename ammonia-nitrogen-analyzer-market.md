<p><p>The Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzer market comprises two primary types: colorimetric and electrode methods. The colorimetric method involves chemical reactions that produce color changes proportional to ammonia concentration, allowing for quantitative analysis. In contrast, the electrode method utilizes ion-selective electrodes to directly measure ammonia ion concentration in a solution. Both methods are essential for water quality monitoring, wastewater treatment, and environmental assessments, catering to various industries that require precise ammonia nitrogen measurements for regulatory compliance and process optimization.</p></p>
