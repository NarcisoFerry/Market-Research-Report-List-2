<p><p>The piezo buzzer components market is divided into two main types: active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers contain an integrated oscillator, allowing them to produce sound when powered, making them easy to use and requiring only DC voltage. In contrast, passive piezo buzzers must be driven by an external audio signal, enabling them to generate varied tones and frequencies. Both types find applications in alarms, indicators, and communication devices, serving distinct functional needs in electronic systems.</p></p>
