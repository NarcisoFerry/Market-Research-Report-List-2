<p><p>The pulp digester market is categorized into three main types: tower, tube, and others. Tower digesters are vertical vessels that facilitate high-pressure cooking of wood chips in a continuous flow, maximizing efficiency. Tube digesters utilize horizontal tubes for a more compact design, promoting rapid cooking and improved process control. Other types include batch digesters and combination systems that integrate various technologies. Each type serves specific production needs, efficiency levels, and space constraints within the pulp manufacturing industry.</p></p>
